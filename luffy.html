"I'm gonna be King of the Pirates! If I die trying, then at least I tried!"

Monkey D. Luffy is the captain of the Straw Hat Pirates and the user of the Gomu Gomu no Mi (Rubber-Rubber Fruit). He possesses the unique ability to stretch his body like rubber. But his obsession with meat might be more dangerous than his enemies!

Iconic Transformations

  1. Gear Second: Enhanced speed and power through increased blood flow
  2. Gear Third: Giant limbs for devastating attacks
  3. Gear Fourth: Boundman: Bouncy, powerful, and nearly unstoppable
  4. Gear Fourth: Snakeman: Increased speed and attack trajectory manipulation
  5. Gear Fifth: Nika Awakening: Cartoonish, god-like freedom of movement
